# What is TinkerCAD?

## What Is "Tinkercad"?

### [Tinkercad](https://www.tinkercad.com/) is a simple, online 3D design and 3D printing app for everyone.

Tinkercad is used to make toys, prototypes, home decor, Minecraft models, jewelry and many more things.
